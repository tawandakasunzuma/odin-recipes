Mandarin Melktert

Description

This South African twist on a custard tart is made with a sweet milk filling and a sprinkling of cinnamon.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 3 cups (750ml) jersey milk or regular milk
  • 1 vanilla bean (split, seeds scraped)
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 1 mandarin
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp cake flour
  • 1/2 cup (110g) caster sugar
  • Ground cinnamon (to dust)
  • Icing sugar (to dust)

Pastry Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups (185g) cake flour
  • 1/4 cup (55g) caster sugar
  • 100g cold unsalted butter (chopped)
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten (plus 1 extra yolk)
  • 2 tsp milk
  • Finely grated zest of 1 mandarin
